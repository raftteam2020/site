Target Groups for the Campaign

MATHEMATICALLY AND ECONOMICALLY FOCUSED HIGH SCHOOL STUDENTS

POTENTIAL GAIN FROM COMPETITION:

  • Shift economics and social sciences students to IT students
  • In total 129203 students out of 298696 in 2017 appeared to choose out of the following three degrees:
  • Economics and social sciences
  • Human sciences
  • Information and communication technologies

MEDICALLY FOCUSED HIGH SCHOOL STUDENTS

POTENTIAL GAIN FROM COMPETITION:

  • Shift medical students to IT students, especially women
  • Since female form more than 60% in attending 
    students in medical and nursing degrees, the wage opportunities in technical degrees offer much better life prospects 

SCIENTIFICALLY FOCUSED HIGH SCHOOL STUDENTS

POTENTIAL GAIN FROM COMPETITION:

  • Keep a steady increase in the number of IT students
  • Since there is an evident decrease in the demand of technical degrees, theres is still plenty of room to increase the number of high school applicants
