Key Performance Indicators

Social Media Engagement

The analytics we’ll need for this metric are often available through the social media platforms prg.ai chooses to employ (like Facebook, for example), and can show how well your social media department is performing 

Participation on Educational & Networking Activities 

The success of educational workshops, networking activities etc. can be measured by the amount of students who participate regularly rather than the total number of participants. 

Percentage of Schools Involved in the Various Activities

With the ambitious plan of attracting 30,559 students, a huge amount of schools must be involved. It is recommended that we build an all-country network with some schools in every region rather than making a strong campaign in few big cities. 

Sex Ratio 

The ratio of women attracted by campaign should be close to 50%. However, this is unattainable when considering only a 5 years horizon and without more profound educational changes (on primary school level for instance). 

Evaluation

Over the past decade we have seen the popularity of podcasts, webinars and any form of online learning rise – a trend only exacerbated by the current pandemic.

The number of initially attracted students should be reasonably high with funnel conversion rates close to 20 % (generally considered as the maximum realistically attainable rates) as studies show that majority of student dropouts happen when students do not find their (online) course relevant which is not likely to happen in our case, since the activities we have presented effectively merge theory with practice and overcome the current obstacles (fear of the unknown, stereotypes about ICT students/the general environment). Benchmark: Surveys shows that the implementation of compass technologies is on the rise in Estonia with about 90% of schools being more or less actively involved.
